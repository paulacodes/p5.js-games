Description of Game

For my whack-a-mole assignment, I decided to use one of America's most infamous families: The Kardashians.

Scoring

Kim is worth the highest number of points, 5, whereas all of the other Kardashians give the player 2 points when hit. There is one mole that lowers the player's score by 5 points, Blac Chyna (Blac is about to become a Kardashian by marrying the sisters' brother, Rob Kardashian, and the sisters aren't very happy about that).

Winning/Losing the game

In order to win, you have to score 50 points. If you've been playing for 60 seconds but the score is still under 50, you lose the game.

Soundtrack

The song used in the game is Kim's first (and hopefully last) released single, Jam (Turn it up) , that has been described as worse than Rebecca Black's Friday.
